Our story

Started on a kitchen table in Hyderabad

Sri began painting Cheriyal-style scrolls as a way to keep an old family craft alive. What started as gifts for friends slowly grew into Kalalokam — a small studio working with local artisans across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

Every product on this site is made by hand, in limited quantities, using natural pigments, terracotta clay, and hand-loomed cloth wherever possible.
